The Effect of Cloud Cover on the Availability of Sentinel-2 Images for Maize Fields

Tools

Google Earth Engine (JavaScript), QGIS, Tableau, etc.

Cloud Cover Effect Explorer

This webpage is based on my thesis for the MSc degree in Sustainable Agriculture at the University of Padova, "The Effect of Cloud Cover on the Availability of Sentinel-2 Images for Maize Fields." It assists stakeholders in determining whether their maize fields can benefit from Sentinel-2, which areas can benefit the most, and to what extent cloud cover affects the availability of Sentinel-2 images for maize fields.
